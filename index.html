Delete Your Traceqi Account

We value your privacy. When you request to delete your account, the following happens:

  • Your profile (Name, Bio, Images) is permanently removed from S3 and our database.
  • Your search and interaction history is erased.
  • All active access requests are canceled.

How to delete:

1. Open the Traceqi app > Go to Profile.

2. Tap the Delete Account button at the bottom.

Alternatively, email us at traceqiteam@gmail.com from your registered email address, and we will process your request within 48 hours.
