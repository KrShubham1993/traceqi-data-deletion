Privacy Policy for Traceqi

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Traceqi. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application to connect with people you've crossed paths with.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and Instagram ID only if you explicitly provide them during account creation or profile updates.
  • Location Data: We do not track your live location in the background. We only collect location data that you manually enter into our search form to filter relevant matches.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your data solely to:

  • Perform filtering operations to match users present at the same location/time.
  • Notify you when a match is found.
  • Facilitate connection requests between users.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Google Analytics to monitor app performance. These parties only have access to data necessary to perform their tasks and are contractually obligated to keep it confidential.

5. Data Retention, Security, and Deletion

Security: We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data during transmission.

Retention: We retain your data only as long as your account is active.

Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" button in the app profile. Alternatively, use the form below:

Request Data Deletion via Email

6. Your Rights (DPDP Act & GDPR)

You have the right to access your data, correct inaccuracies, withdraw your consent for data processing, and request the permanent deletion of your information (the "Right to be Forgotten").

7. Children's Privacy

Traceqi requires users to be at least 13 years of age. We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately for removal.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
