Privacy Policy for Traceqi

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Traceqi. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application to connect with people you've crossed paths with.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and Instagram ID (only if explicitly provided).
  • Location Data: We do not track live background location. We only process location data you manually enter to find relevant matches at specific times.

3. Purpose & Third Parties

We use data solely to perform filtering for matches and facilitate connection requests. We use Google Analytics for performance monitoring; they access only necessary, non-identifiable data under strict confidentiality.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. You have the right to be forgotten.

To delete your account and all associated data (PII, images, and history):
